Who We Are

A Movement Built on
Collective Kindness

InAmigos Foundation was founded by Mr. Govind Shukla with a simple belief — a kinder society is possible when ordinary people take extraordinary action together.

We are a Section 8 non-profit organisation, licensed by the Central Government. Our work spans education, food donation, women empowerment, animal protection, environmental sustainability and youth skill development — all across India.

With a strong network of volunteers, professionals and corporate partners, we work every day to turn compassion into real, lasting change.

ISO 9001:2015 80G Certified 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog NGO Darpan

In a world that often feels divided, InAmigos stands as a beacon of hope — dedicated to fostering unity and support within society. Every contribution, no matter how small, can spark a change that lasts a lifetime.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects. One Mission.

Each project targets a real gap in society. Together they form a complete picture of the change InAmigos wants to create.

🤝

Project SEVA

Distributing food, clothing and essential supplies to homeless and underprivileged communities. Our volunteers regularly organise drives across India to bring comfort to those who need it most.

Food & Clothing
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Providing quality education and learning support to underprivileged children. We create spaces where young minds can grow, dream and build a brighter future — free of cost.

Education
🌸

Project Udaan

Empowering women through vocational training, skill development and financial independence programs. We help women take flight toward a self-reliant, confident life.

Women Empowerment
🌳

Project Prakriti

Working toward environmental conservation through sapling drives, awareness campaigns and sustainability efforts. Every tree we plant is a promise to the future.

Environment
🐾

Project Jeev

Rescuing, sheltering, feeding and caring for animals in need. Our volunteers believe compassion has no limits — it extends to every living being around us.

Animal Welfare
🚀

Project Vikas

Building the next generation of professionals through digital and soft-skills training. Over 30,000 interns trained so far under this flagship programme.

Skill Development
Why It Matters

The Real Change We're Creating

Every initiative addresses a genuine problem in society — from hunger and illiteracy to deforestation and gender inequality.

🍽️

Fighting Hunger

No family should sleep on an empty stomach. Our food drives reach communities that are often completely ignored by the system.

🎓

Education for All

Poverty should never decide who gets an education. Bachpanshala breaks that cycle — one child at a time.

💪

Women's Independence

Skill training and financial literacy give women the tools to build lives they choose — not lives forced upon them.

🌱

A Greener Tomorrow

Deforestation is a crisis. Every sapling we plant with Prakriti is a small step toward healing our environment.

🐕

Protecting Animals

Stray and injured animals deserve care too. Jeev gives them food, shelter and medical attention they can't ask for themselves.

💼

Youth Employment

30,000+ young Indians have gained real skills through Vikas — ready to contribute, lead and succeed in the modern world.
